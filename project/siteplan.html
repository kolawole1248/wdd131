Coffea Website Site Plan

Site Name

Coffea Corner

This name represents a cozy and inviting space for coffee lovers to explore and learn about coffee. It is simple, memorable, and relevant to the project's focus on coffee culture.

Optional domain availability: coffeacorner.com

Site Purpose

The Coffea Corner website provides a hub for coffee enthusiasts to:

  • Discover different types of coffee beans and brewing methods.
  • Learn about the history and culture of coffee.
  • Find recipes for coffee-based drinks and desserts.
  • Explore coffee shops and cafes around the world.

Scenarios

Here are two scenarios that represent the target audience's needs:

  • What are the best brewing methods for a strong espresso?
  • Where can I find a coffee shop near me that serves organic coffee?

Color Schema

The following colors will be used in the website:

#6F4E37 (Coffee Brown)
#C4A484 (Light Coffee)
#F5F5DC (Beige)

Usage:

  • #6F4E37: Headings, buttons, and accents.
  • #C4A484: Background for sections and cards.
  • #F5F5DC: Main background color.

Typography

The following fonts will be used in the website:

Merriweather: Used for headings and titles.

Open Sans: Used for body text and general content.
